Jager

Jager has pioneered several innovative technologies. These include fully customisable software for prepaid electricity meters, an iOS and Android app for smart monitoring and various renewable energy solutions. Jager offers these technologies to individuals and commercial property owners, allowing the latter to recover utility usage costs and, in turn, pay the bulk supplier.

Founded by Thomas Davies, a former Global Director at Google, Temporall helps executives and their leadership teams design and sustain high-performance organisational culture. The Culture Workbench uses Enterprise security, Machine learning and is GDPR compliant.

MORI

Part of 500 Startups’ 17th batch and winner of the Silicon Valley G-Startup Global Finals, MORI is one of the most rapid-growing startups, intent on revolutionising the babywear industry. TechCrunch selected MORI as one of their favourite companies from 500 Startups’ 17th batch.

Bruce’s Doggy Day Care

The UK’s largest dog day care operator - Bruce’s Doggy Day Care (BDDC) is a multi award-winning business which offers personalised day care for pups. Funtalkingca implemented and continues to improve a fully bespoke CRM system to allow customers to make reservations from both web and mobile, integrating into the back office systems and processes of BDDC teams.

Huntsman

Serving as the inspiration and filming location for Matthew Vaughn’s blockbuster movie Kingsman: The Secret Service and Kingsman: The Golden Circle, Huntsman is a high-end fashion house and tailor established in 1849.
