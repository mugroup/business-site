]]> Toshi is an e-commerce solution exclusively for the luxury fashion industry in central London. It integrates with the retailer’s existing e-commerce solution and allows them to optimise inventory, conversion to sales, customer experience, customer retention as well as to minimize returns. They do this by partnering with fashion retailers who want to offer a premium service whereby an assistant is sent to the customer within one hour or when the customer chooses to do a fitting. Upon checkout the Toshi system checks whether the customer falls within the range of the store, and if so, it will show Toshi as a delivery option. Toshi will also then show other sizes available and the customer can let the assistant bring those sizes along for fitting as well.

]]> Bluubin is an exciting cloud accounting & payroll solutions company used by over 2500 businesses. With Bluubin you can manage your business finances from any computer, tablet or smartphone.

We built the entire back end for Bluubin, and most recently, the user management system. This system includes automations such as event-triggered emails based on a user’s actions, for example, when a user signs up, when his profile is left empty after 7 days, how-to guides after 14 and 30 days etc. We also implemented a debit order integration with Nedbank and credit card integration with PayFast. In addition, we implemented automated solutions catered for more complex user management cases such as:

  • Upgrading a certain package of the software while simultaneously downgrading another part
  • Switching from arrears billing to billing in advance
  • Switching from monthly to annual billing
  • Previous versions also included  payment based on certain usage parameters, for example, the number of transactions per customer in a given month, so a customer could receive a bill of R250 one month and automatically get an invoice of R2,500 the next month if the threshold of transactions were exceeded.

By creating a simple, content rich and social environment, the tool boosts productivity and helps remove a major daily headache.

For press houses, their tool allows editors, stylists and influencers to simply browse through samples, thanks to an intelligent search function, and then request them via an integrated calendar showing the availability of each sample.

For brands and PR agencies, PASHION helps increase product exposure through additional requests. It also helps remove friction points throughout the entire sample process thereby increasing productivity internally and externally with the press.

The PASHION tool is a visual communicating platform for the fashion industry to manage sample exchange.

PASHION:

  • Is a one-stop resource with all the media content and information brands/press/PR agencies need
  • Is an integrated messaging system allows users to identify, connect and engage with players in the industry
  • Gathers data on sample trends to feed into editorial, production and marketing decisions
  • Allows you to share, make a request or open a discussion about any sample with a simple visual sharing tool
  • Allows you to create shipping requests in one click using the deep integration with our local expert courier companies
  • Can track your sample requests anytime, anywhere with web based and mobile tracking tools.
